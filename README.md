# **AgentKon**: A Google Gemini Enterprise, ADK & MCP Hackathon 🚀

Welcome to **AgentKon**, our hands-on hackathon dedicated to exploring and building with Google's powerful agent development tools: **Gemini Enterprise**, the **Agent Development Kit (ADK)**, and **Multi-agent Control Plane (MCP)**.

This repository contains the agenda and resources for our session. Throughout the day, you'll participate in four guided labs and a final group brainstorming exercise to apply your new skills. Here is a sample agenda for the day.

> [!NOTE]
> Your Google Cloud team is also eager to tailor this event to meet your needs more specifically. Please let us know if you have any requests or suggestions by reaching out to your Google Cloud CE or account manager.

## 🧠 Group Exercise: Brainstorming

To conclude the day, you will form groups and brainstorm a practical application for the technologies covered.

### 🎯 Your Goal:

1.  **Identify a use case** within your company where agent technology could be applied.
2.  **Analyze the impact:** How could this solution **reduce expenses** or **increase revenue**?
3.  **Prepare a pitch:** Create a [one-slider](https://docs.google.com/presentation) outlining your idea.
4.  **Present:** Each group will select one person to present the idea.
